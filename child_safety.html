Child Safety and Age Verification

Effective Date: 20th January 2025

1. Age Verification

At Cyrcl, we are committed to ensuring the safety of all users, particularly minors. Our platform requires users to be at least 16 years old to create an account. We implement the following measures to verify user age:

  • During registration, users must confirm they are at least 16 years old
  • We may request additional verification if we suspect a user is underage
  • Parents or guardians can request account deletion for underage users

2. Parental Controls

We provide tools and resources for parents to monitor and manage their children's use of our platform:

  • Account monitoring capabilities for parents
  • Content filtering options
  • Time management features
  • Reporting mechanisms for inappropriate content or behavior

3. Content Safety

We maintain strict guidelines to ensure all content is appropriate for our user base:

  • Automated content filtering systems
  • Human moderation of reported content
  • Clear community guidelines
  • Zero tolerance for harmful or inappropriate content

4. Reporting and Enforcement

Users can report inappropriate content or behavior through multiple channels:

  • In-app reporting system
  • Email support
  • 24/7 moderation team

We take all reports seriously and investigate them promptly.

5. Educational Resources

We provide educational resources for both parents and young users:

  • Online safety guides
  • Digital citizenship resources
  • Parental control tutorials
  • Regular safety updates and tips

6. Compliance

We comply with all relevant laws and regulations regarding child safety and online protection, including:

  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • Other applicable local and international regulations
